Research Works

post-quantum cryptography algorithms

This research evaluates NIST’s newly standardized post-quantum cryptography algorithms (Kyber, Dilithium, and SPHINCS+) against classical schemes like RSA and ECDSA. Using open-source implementations, it benchmarks performance and analyzes vulnerabilities such as misuse, randomness failures, and timing leaks. The results provide practical guidance for developers and system integrators transitioning to PQC.
